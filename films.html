Keanu Reeves Filmography

Iconic Action Films

The Matrix (1999)

Groundbreaking sci-fi action film that redefined special effects and philosophy in cinema. Reeves' portrayal of Neo became iconic.

John Wick (2014)

Revolutionary action thriller that showcased Reeves' dedication to practical stunts and intense fight choreography.

Speed (1994)

High-octane thriller that established Reeves as a leading action star with its tense plot and memorable performances.

Cult Classics & Indies

Bill & Ted's Excellent Adventure (1989)

Beloved time-travel comedy that made Reeves a household name with its charming humor and positive message.

My Own Private Idaho (1991)

Independent drama showcasing Reeves' dramatic range as a narcoleptic street hustler in this Gus Van Sant masterpiece.

Point Break (1991)

Action thriller featuring extreme sports and bank robbery, demonstrating Reeves' ability in adrenaline-fueled roles.

Dramatic Roles

A Scanner Darkly (2006)

Animated psychological thriller exploring drug addiction and surveillance, featuring Reeves in a complex dramatic role.

The Devil's Advocate (1997)

Supernatural thriller co-starring Al Pacino, showcasing Reeves' performance in a morally complex narrative.

Constantine (2005)

Supernatural horror film based on DC Comics' Hellblazer, featuring Reeves as the titular exorcist.

Recent Successes

John Wick: Chapter 2 (2017)

Expanded the John Wick universe with even more intricate action sequences and world-building.

John Wick: Chapter 3 – Parabellum (2019)

Continued the acclaimed series with record-breaking opening weekend and spectacular action choreography.

The Matrix Reloaded (2003)

Sequel expanding the Matrix universe with more complex philosophy and groundbreaking action sequences.
